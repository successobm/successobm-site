FAQ

Questions about SuccessOBM features

Core execution capabilities include task creation and management, contact and lead record management, quote and invoice creation, newsletter and campaign sending, and email outreach connected to contact records. You can connect a Gmail account to send mail from that address through the platform (OAuth; send-only; no inbox sync). Trigger-based workflow automation, SMS, social media management, and agent-based execution are being built as part of the platform's near-term direction. Where a feature is in progress, we say so rather than presenting the platform as something it isn't yet.

Contacts in SuccessOBM aren't a separate database — they're the records that every other platform activity connects to. A contact can be a lead in your sales pipeline, a subscriber on your marketing list, a client with task history and invoices, or all of the above. When you send an email, create a task, or generate a quote, it ties back to the contact record. That means everyone on your team is working from the same information, not pulling from separate tools.

Automation in SuccessOBM works on a trigger-and-action model: when something happens in the platform — a form is submitted, a quote is sent, a task is completed — a defined set of actions runs automatically. That might be sending a follow-up email, creating a task for a team member, moving a lead to the next pipeline stage, or starting a new workflow. The goal is that repeatable business tasks run consistently without someone manually managing each step. This capability is actively being built out.

For businesses using a standalone quoting or invoicing tool that's separate from where they manage contacts and tasks, SuccessOBM is designed to replace that separation. Quotes and invoices are created inside the platform, tied to the client record, and followed up on as tasks — so the full loop from new business to payment lives in one place. Payment gateway integration and advanced billing features are part of the platform direction as it develops.

Task management, contact and lead records, quote and invoice creation, newsletter sending, and Gmail send (connected account, OAuth) are the current platform foundation. Trigger-based workflow automation, SMS outreach, social media management, and agent-based task execution are actively in development. The AI Master Agent is being built as a task coordination and execution interface across the platform. We've tried to be straightforward throughout this page about what's live, what's in progress, and what's part of the direction — because that's more useful than pretending everything is already built.
