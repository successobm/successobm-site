Capture Work

Every business task starts with capturing it

New work arrives as form submissions, contact inquiries, service requests, and new business leads. The moment it lands, SuccessOBM turns it into an actionable record — assigned, tracked, and ready to move, not sitting in an inbox waiting to be noticed.

A form submission becomes a contact record with a task attached. An inquiry becomes a lead in your pipeline with a next step defined. Nothing sits in a holding state — incoming activity enters the platform as work, with ownership and status from the start.

Capture Work

How new work enters the platform

  • Business forms and intake submissions
  • Contact and lead capture from inquiries
  • New business requests turned into platform records
  • Gmail send — outbound email from a connected Gmail account (OAuth)
  • Subscriber and audience capture for marketing lists

Organize Work

What each task record contains

  • Owner — who is responsible for this task
  • Status — open, in progress, overdue, or complete
  • Next step — what happens before this closes
  • Deadline — due date with overdue flagging
  • Contact context — the client or lead this work belongs to
  • Role visibility — staff queue vs. full owner view

Organize Work

Every task has an owner, a status, and a next step

A captured inquiry becomes a task record — with an owner, a status, a deadline, and a defined next step. Your team sees what's assigned to them. You see what's open, what's overdue, and what's stalled across the whole business.

Staff see their own queue. Owners and admins see the full picture. Filter by status, owner, or business area to find what needs attention — without chasing people or digging through email threads.

Execute Work

This is where the work actually gets done

Execution is the heart of SuccessOBM. Every task has an owner, a status, a deadline, and a next step. Agents help draft, schedule, remind, and follow up — so business work moves forward without someone manually pushing each step. Email outreach, newsletter sends, social posts, client follow-up, and operational tasks all run through the same system.

Send individual emails and SMS directly from a contact record — with the history, context, and next step visible alongside the message. Agents can draft follow-ups and send reminders on a defined schedule, so outreach keeps moving without manual tracking at every step.

Social Media

Each post is a task — with an assigned owner, a review step, and a scheduled publish date. Agents can draft content based on topic or campaign context. No clipboard-to-scheduler workflows, no disconnected content calendar tools. Social content runs through the same task system as everything else.

Automate & Scale

Real business events trigger the next step automatically

A new contact arrives — a follow-up task starts. A quote goes out — a reminder fires if it isn't opened. A job completes — the invoice task is created. SuccessOBM's automation is built around the actual events in your task system, not a generic workflow builder you have to configure from scratch.

Workflow Automation

Trigger-based automation, task templates, and agent execution

Define what happens when a business event fires — a form submitted, a quote sent, a task marked complete — and the platform runs the next step. Task templates make repeatable work consistent. Agents execute the steps that run across contacts, tasks, and communications at scale.

  • Triggers fire on platform events — form submitted, quote sent, task complete
  • Task templates — same steps, same owner, same sequence every time
  • Growing task library — more automatable business tasks added over time
  • Agents run the steps that repeat — follow-ups, reminders, drafts, routing

Follow-up & Outreach Agents

When a quote goes out, a reminder fires on day three if it hasn't been opened. When a job closes, a check-in goes out on schedule. Follow-up tasks that would otherwise be missed run automatically — triggered by what actually happened in the task system.

Workflow Step Execution

Workflows advance on their own — assigning the next task to the right owner, moving a lead to the next stage, sending a status update to the client. Each step runs based on what completed before it, not on someone remembering to check.

Master Agent — Connected to the Task Engine

The Master Agent works inside the task system — not alongside it. Ask it to surface what's overdue, kick off a follow-up sequence, route a new inquiry to the right owner, or check what's outstanding on a client account. It's being built as the task execution interface across the whole platform.

FAQ

Questions about SuccessOBM features

Core execution capabilities include task creation and management, contact and lead record management, quote and invoice creation, newsletter and campaign sending, and email outreach connected to contact records. You can connect a Gmail account to send mail from that address through the platform (OAuth; send-only; no inbox sync). Trigger-based workflow automation, SMS, social media management, and agent-based execution are being built as part of the platform's near-term direction. Where a feature is in progress, we say so rather than presenting the platform as something it isn't yet.

Contacts in SuccessOBM aren't a separate database — they're the records that every other platform activity connects to. A contact can be a lead in your sales pipeline, a subscriber on your marketing list, a client with task history and invoices, or all of the above. When you send an email, create a task, or generate a quote, it ties back to the contact record. That means everyone on your team is working from the same information, not pulling from separate tools.

Automation in SuccessOBM works on a trigger-and-action model: when something happens in the platform — a form is submitted, a quote is sent, a task is completed — a defined set of actions runs automatically. That might be sending a follow-up email, creating a task for a team member, moving a lead to the next pipeline stage, or starting a new workflow. The goal is that repeatable business tasks run consistently without someone manually managing each step. This capability is actively being built out.

For businesses using a standalone quoting or invoicing tool that's separate from where they manage contacts and tasks, SuccessOBM is designed to replace that separation. Quotes and invoices are created inside the platform, tied to the client record, and followed up on as tasks — so the full loop from new business to payment lives in one place. Payment gateway integration and advanced billing features are part of the platform direction as it develops.

Task management, contact and lead records, quote and invoice creation, newsletter sending, and Gmail send (connected account, OAuth) are the current platform foundation. Trigger-based workflow automation, SMS outreach, social media management, and agent-based task execution are actively in development. The AI Master Agent is being built as a task coordination and execution interface across the platform. We've tried to be straightforward throughout this page about what's live, what's in progress, and what's part of the direction — because that's more useful than pretending everything is already built.
